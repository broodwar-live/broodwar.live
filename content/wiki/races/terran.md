The Terran are human exiles from Earth, scraping by on the fringes of the galaxy. Their military combines conventional ballistic weaponry, powered armor, and nuclear technology. Terran buildings can lift off and relocate, and many units can be repaired by SCVs. The faction excels at positional play, turtling behind bunkers and siege tanks, then transitioning into powerful late-game armies with Battlecruisers or mech compositions.
