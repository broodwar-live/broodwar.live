The Protoss are an ancient alien race with immense psionic abilities and advanced technology. Their units are individually powerful but expensive, protected by regenerating plasma shields on top of their base hit points. Protoss buildings must be placed within power fields generated by Pylons, making power management a critical aspect of play. Their warp-in mechanic means buildings construct themselves once warped in, freeing Probes to keep mining.
