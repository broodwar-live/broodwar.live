Disruption Web is a Corsair ability that creates an energy field on the ground lasting approximately 20 seconds. Any ground unit or structure within the field is unable to attack, though they can still move. The field affects both enemy and friendly units, so careful placement is required. Air units are unaffected and can continue attacking normally.

In competitive play, Disruption Web is a core component of the Corsair-Reaver PvZ strategy. Corsairs cast Disruption Web on Spore Colonies and anti-air defenses, neutralizing them so Corsairs can freely fly over and Reavers can bombard without retaliation. In PvT, it can disable Siege Tanks and Missile Turrets, creating openings for ground assaults. The ability turns static defense investments into liabilities and forces opponents to either move their units out of the web or wait helplessly while being attacked.

Disruption Web is researched at the Fleet Beacon, typically in mid-game Corsair-focused builds. In PvZ Corsair-Reaver play, it is available around the 10-12 minute mark. The upgrade is rarely seen outside of air-based strategies, as it requires significant investment in the Stargate tech path.
