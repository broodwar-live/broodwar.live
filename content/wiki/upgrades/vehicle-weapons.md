Each level of Vehicle Weapons adds +1 base attack damage to Vultures, Siege Tanks, and Goliaths. Researched at the Armory, with higher levels requiring a Science Facility. Because Siege Tanks deal splash damage, the per-shot increase is effectively multiplied across all units caught in the blast radius, making this upgrade disproportionately powerful.

Vehicle Weapons is the top priority upgrade in Terran mech compositions, particularly in TvP. A +1 Siege Tank hits critical damage thresholds that allow it to destroy key Protoss units in fewer volleys. In TvZ mech play, upgraded Vulture and Tank damage accelerates the ability to hold expansions and push across the map. Pros prioritize keeping the Armory researching continuously once mech play is committed.

In standard mech builds, the first Vehicle Weapons level begins as soon as the Armory completes, typically around 8-9 minutes. The upgrade is continuously researched through mid-game into late-game. Reaching +3 Vehicle Weapons before a major push is a common timing goal for mech players.
