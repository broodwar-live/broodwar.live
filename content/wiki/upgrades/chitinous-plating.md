Chitinous Plating adds +2 armor to Ultralisks, stacking on top of their base 1 armor and any Carapace upgrades from the Evolution Chamber. With full upgrades (Chitinous Plating + level 3 Carapace), an Ultralisk reaches 6 total armor, reducing each incoming hit by 6 damage. This makes Ultralisks extraordinarily tanky against rapid, low-damage attacks like Marine fire and Zergling swarms, where most of each hit is absorbed by armor.

In competitive play, Chitinous Plating is essential for making Ultralisks effective. Without it, Ultralisks take too much damage from massed small-arms fire and die before contributing meaningful damage. With full armor, Ultralisks become nearly impervious to Marine fire — each Marine shot deals only 1-2 damage after armor reduction, requiring enormous volumes of fire to bring them down. Under Dark Swarm where Marines cannot shoot, armored Ultralisks are virtually unkillable by ground forces.

Chitinous Plating is researched at the Ultralisk Cavern, typically as soon as it completes, around the 15-18 minute mark in games reaching Hive tech. It is always researched alongside Anabolic Synthesis (speed), as both upgrades are required for Ultralisks to function at their full potential in the late game.
