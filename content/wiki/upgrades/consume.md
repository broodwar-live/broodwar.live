Consume is a Defiler ability that instantly kills a friendly Zerg unit to restore 50 energy to the Defiler. The sacrificed unit is destroyed immediately regardless of its remaining HP. Any Zerg unit can be consumed, but Zerglings are the most common choice since they cost only 25 minerals for a pair — making them the cheapest energy battery available.

Consume is essential for sustaining Defiler spell usage in the late game. Without it, Defilers are limited to one or two spell casts before running out of energy, severely limiting their impact. With Consume, a Defiler accompanied by a stream of Zerglings can cast Dark Swarm and Plague repeatedly, maintaining constant spell pressure. This enables the Zerg late-game strategy of pushing under Dark Swarm while Plaguing enemy units — a combination that defines ZvT and ZvP late game.

Consume is researched at the Defiler Mound, typically as soon as it completes, around the 14-16 minute mark in games that reach Hive tech. It is always researched alongside or shortly before the first Defilers emerge. Without this upgrade, Defilers are not worth producing — the energy limitation makes them ineffective.
