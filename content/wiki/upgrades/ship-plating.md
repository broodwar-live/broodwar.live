Each level of Ship Plating adds +1 armor to all Terran air units, including Wraiths, Valkyries, Battlecruisers, Dropships, and Science Vessels. Higher armor reduces incoming damage per hit, improving survivability against anti-air fire. Researched at the Armory, with higher levels requiring a Science Facility.

Ship Plating is very rarely researched in competitive Brood War. Standard games revolve around ground compositions — bio or mech — and air units typically serve support roles where plating offers minimal value. The upgrade only becomes relevant in extreme late-game scenarios featuring Battlecruiser fleets or in unusual air-transition strategies. Even in games with significant Wraith or Valkyrie usage, ship weapons and other upgrades take priority over plating.

When researched at all, Ship Plating appears only in the very late game, well after ground upgrades are maxed and the Terran player has transitioned heavily into air units. In practice, fewer than 5% of competitive games see this upgrade researched.
