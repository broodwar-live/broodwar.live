Each level of Infantry Armor adds +1 armor to Marines, Firebats, Ghosts, and Medics. Higher armor reduces incoming damage per hit, making it most effective against rapid, low-damage attacks such as Zergling strikes and Mutalisk bounces. The upgrade is researched at the Engineering Bay, with higher levels requiring a Science Facility.

In competitive play, Infantry Armor is generally considered secondary to Infantry Weapons but remains important for bio survivability in extended engagements. The armor upgrade reduces the effectiveness of splash damage sources and helps Marines survive longer under Medic healing. Against Zerg, armor upgrades become increasingly valuable as the game progresses and Zergling counts rise. In TvT bio mirrors, the armor advantage can decide Marine vs Marine trades.

Infantry Armor is usually started after the first level of Infantry Weapons, around mid-game. Some pros will alternate weapon and armor levels to maintain a balanced upgrade advantage. In very long macro games, reaching +3/+3 weapons and armor is a critical power spike for Terran bio armies.
