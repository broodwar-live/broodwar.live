Lurker Aspect enables Hydralisks to morph into Lurkers, one of Zerg's most important defensive units. Lurkers burrow underground and become invisible, attacking by sending subterranean spines in a line that deals 20 damage to all ground units caught in the path. They cannot move while burrowed and require detection to be targeted. The morphing process takes several seconds and consumes the Hydralisk. Requires Lair tech.

Lurker Aspect is one of the most critical Zerg tech unlocks, particularly in ZvT. Lurkers are the primary defensive unit against Terran bio pushes — burrowed Lurkers behind choke points force Terran to invest in Science Vessel detection and careful siege positioning before advancing. Without Lurkers, Zerg lacks a reliable way to hold ground against stimmed Marine and Medic armies. In ZvP, Lurkers are effective against Zealot-heavy compositions and can protect expansions from ground attacks.

Lurker Aspect is researched at the Hydralisk Den as soon as Lair completes, typically around the 8-10 minute mark in standard ZvT builds. This is one of the most time-sensitive upgrades in the matchup — delayed Lurkers can result in losing to early Terran pushes. In ZvP, the timing is more flexible depending on the specific build order.
