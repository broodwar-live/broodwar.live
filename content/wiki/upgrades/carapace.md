Each level of Carapace adds +1 armor to all Zerg ground units, including Zerglings, Hydralisks, Lurkers, Ultralisks, and Drones. Higher armor reduces incoming damage per hit, which is most impactful against rapid, low-damage attacks. Researched at the Evolution Chamber, with higher levels requiring Lair and Hive respectively.

Carapace is often the first upgrade Zerg researches, even before attack upgrades. The critical breakpoint is that +1 Carapace Zerglings survive one additional Marine shot, changing the exchange ratio dramatically in early and mid-game engagements. This single upgrade level can mean the difference between Zerglings dying before reaching Marines and Zerglings reaching melee range with enough HP to fight. In ZvT, the +1 Carapace timing is one of the most important benchmarks in the matchup. In ZvP, armored Zerglings are significantly harder for Zealots and Probes to clean up.

Carapace is typically started at the Evolution Chamber around the 5-7 minute mark, often as the very first upgrade in ZvT. Continuous upgrading through all three levels is standard, with many Zerg players prioritizing Carapace over attack upgrades at each level.
