Irradiate is a Science Vessel ability that deals 250 damage over 30 seconds to a targeted biological unit and splashes to nearby biological units within a small radius. The targeted unit glows green and damages all adjacent biological allies, making it devastating against clumped air units. Irradiate cannot kill a unit directly — it reduces HP to a minimum of 1.

Irradiate defines the TvZ mid-to-late game. A single cast on a Mutalisk in a clumped flock can kill multiple Mutalisks due to the splash effect, making it the primary counter to Mutalisk harassment. It is also effective against Overlords, denying Zerg scouting and supply, and against clumped Hydralisks or Zerglings. In TvP, Irradiate sees occasional use against Zealot-heavy armies. The ability forces opponents to spread their biological units or lose them rapidly.

Irradiate is researched at the Science Facility, typically as soon as the first Science Vessel is produced in TvZ, around the 10-12 minute mark. It is a mid-game timing that signals Terran's transition to a more dominant position against Zerg air play.
