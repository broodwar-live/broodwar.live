Muscular Augments significantly increases Hydralisk movement speed, addressing one of the unit's biggest weaknesses — its sluggish base speed. Without this upgrade, Hydralisks are too slow to engage effectively, retreat from unfavorable fights, or reposition during battles. With it, Hydralisks become mobile enough to serve as a core army component, capable of kiting slower melee units and retreating from danger.

In competitive play, Muscular Augments is essential whenever Hydralisks are a significant part of the army composition. In ZvP, speed Hydralisks can engage and disengage against Dragoon-Zealot armies, choosing favorable fights rather than being forced into bad engagements. In ZvT, faster Hydralisks can support Lurker positions more effectively and retreat before being surrounded. Without this upgrade, Hydralisk-based strategies are simply not viable at the competitive level.

Muscular Augments is researched at the Hydralisk Den, usually alongside or immediately after Grooved Spines (range upgrade). In Hydralisk-focused builds, both upgrades are started as soon as the Den completes, around the 7-9 minute mark. The two upgrades are considered a package deal — researching one without the other leaves Hydralisks significantly weakened.
