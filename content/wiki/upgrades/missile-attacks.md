Each level of Missile Attacks adds +1 base ranged damage to Hydralisks and Lurkers. For Hydralisks, the damage increase improves their output in direct combat against both ground and air targets. For Lurkers, increased damage from their subterranean spines makes burrowed positions far more lethal — each spine hit on units walking through the attack line deals more damage. Researched at the Evolution Chamber, with higher levels requiring Lair and Hive.

In competitive play, Missile Attacks is critical in ZvP Hydralisk-based builds. Upgraded Hydralisks trade significantly better against Dragoons and can threaten Corsairs more effectively. Higher Lurker damage from missile upgrades makes entrenched Lurker positions progressively harder to break, particularly for Terran trying to push through fortified positions. In ZvT, upgraded Lurkers behind choke points become devastating, requiring more Siege Tanks or Science Vessel detection to dislodge.

Missile Attacks timing depends on the composition. In Hydralisk-heavy ZvP builds, the first level begins as soon as the Evolution Chamber completes, around 7-8 minutes. In ZvT with Lurker play, it is researched alongside Carapace upgrades through mid-game. The upgrade is less relevant in Zergling-focused strategies.
