U-238 Shells increases Marine attack range by 1 (from 4 to 5), giving Marines a significant reach advantage in combat. This extra range allows Marines to fire from further back in formation, reducing the number of units that can be hit by splash damage simultaneously and improving overall engagement efficiency.

In competitive bio play, U-238 Shells is critical. The additional range lets Marines kite Zealots more effectively, outrange Lurkers with careful micro, and engage Zerglings from a safer distance. In TvT, the range advantage can determine which player wins Marine vs Marine skirmishes. The upgrade also improves Marine effectiveness when fighting from high ground or behind chokepoints, where the extra reach means more Marines can contribute their damage.

U-238 Shells is typically researched at the Academy right after or alongside Stim Pack, around the 6-7 minute mark. Some build orders prioritize it before Stim in matchups where the range advantage is more immediately useful, such as certain TvP openings where kiting Zealots is the top priority.
