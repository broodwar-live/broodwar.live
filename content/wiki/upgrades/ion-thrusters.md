Ion Thrusters significantly increases Vulture movement speed, making Vultures the fastest ground unit in the game. The speed boost transforms Vultures into elite hit-and-run harassers capable of outrunning every ground unit and escaping unfavorable engagements with ease. Combined with their Spider Mines, speed-upgraded Vultures become one of the most efficient harassment tools available.

In competitive play, Ion Thrusters is essential for Vulture-based strategies, particularly in TvZ. Fast Vultures can run circles around Zerglings, pick off Drones at expansions, and plant mines deep in enemy territory before retreating safely. The speed advantage also allows Vultures to kite Zealots in TvP and scout the map rapidly. Without this upgrade, Vultures lack the mobility to perform their harassment role effectively.

Ion Thrusters is typically researched at the Machine Shop in TvZ after Spider Mines and Siege Tech, around the 8-10 minute mark. In some aggressive Vulture-first builds, it may come earlier. The upgrade is a mid-game investment that pays dividends throughout the rest of the match.
