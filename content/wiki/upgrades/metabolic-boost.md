Metabolic Boost dramatically increases Zergling movement speed, transforming them from relatively slow units into one of the fastest ground units in the game. Speed Zerglings can chase down nearly any ground unit, escape unfavorable engagements, and execute devastating run-bys into enemy bases. The speed difference between upgraded and non-upgraded Zerglings is enormous and fundamentally changes how the unit is used.

Metabolic Boost is researched in virtually every Zerg game — it is one of the most universally important upgrades in Brood War. In ZvZ, the timing of Zergling speed defines the early game: the classic 9-Pool Speed build aims to get speed Zerglings as quickly as possible for aggression. In ZvT, speed Zerglings are essential for run-bys against expansions and for surrounding Siege Tank positions. In ZvP, speed Zerglings threaten Probes and force Protoss to wall off or invest in Zealot defense.

Metabolic Boost is researched at the Spawning Pool, with the timing varying from extremely early (5-6 minutes in speed-first builds) to slightly delayed (7-8 minutes in economy-first builds). Regardless of the exact timing, it is almost never skipped. Speed Zerglings remain relevant throughout the entire game.
