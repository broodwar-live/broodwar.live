Each level of Air Weapons adds +1 base attack damage to Scouts, Corsairs, Carriers, and Arbiters. The upgrade is especially impactful for Carriers because each Interceptor benefits individually — a Carrier launching 8 Interceptors effectively gains +8 damage per attack cycle per level. For Corsairs, upgraded damage improves their ability to quickly dispatch Mutalisks and Scourge. Researched at the Cybernetics Core, with higher levels requiring a Fleet Beacon.

In competitive play, Air Weapons is critical when committing to Carrier-based strategies in PvT or PvZ. The multiplicative effect across Interceptors makes each upgrade level exponentially more valuable for Carrier fleets. For Corsair-heavy PvZ strategies, air weapon upgrades increase the efficiency of Corsair swarms against Mutalisk harass, allowing fewer Corsairs to achieve the same kills.

Air Weapons timing depends entirely on the strategy. In Corsair-Reaver PvZ builds, it may be started around 10-12 minutes. In Carrier transitions, it begins when the Fleet Beacon completes, typically in the mid-to-late game. Standard gateway-focused builds skip this upgrade entirely.
