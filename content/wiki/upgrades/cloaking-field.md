Cloaking Field enables Wraiths to become invisible, consuming energy while active. Cloaked Wraiths cannot be targeted by enemy units without detection (Observers, Overlords, Science Vessels, or static detectors). The cloak drains energy over time, so Wraiths must periodically decloak or find a safe area when energy runs low. While cloaked, Wraiths can freely harass workers and snipe key buildings.

In competitive play, Cloaking Field is a niche but effective upgrade used primarily for economic harassment. Cloaked Wraiths picking off workers force the opponent to invest in detection at every base, diverting resources from army production. The upgrade sees the most use in TvT, where a surprise Wraith cloak transition can devastate an unprepared opponent, and in TvZ, where cloaked Wraiths can kill Overlords and harass mineral lines. It is less common in TvP due to the ubiquity of Observers.

Cloaking Field is researched at the Control Tower, typically in specialized builds that commit to Wraith play. The timing varies widely — it can come as early as 8 minutes in aggressive Wraith openings or much later as a mid-game tech switch.
