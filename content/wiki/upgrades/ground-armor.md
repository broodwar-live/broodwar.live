Each level of Ground Armor adds +1 armor to Zealots, Dragoons, Dark Templar, and other Protoss ground units. Higher armor reduces incoming damage per hit, which is particularly valuable for Zealots that absorb heavy fire while closing distance to melee range. Researched at the Forge, with higher levels requiring a Templar Archives.

Ground Armor is important for Zealot survivability in all matchups. Armored Zealots take less damage from rapid-fire attacks like Marine volleys and Zergling swarms, allowing them to reach melee range with more HP remaining. In PvP, armor upgrades make Zealots significantly harder to kill in the early gateway battles. Combined with Plasma Shields, armor creates layers of damage reduction that compound in effectiveness during prolonged engagements.

Ground Armor is usually researched after the first level of Ground Weapons, starting in the mid-game around 8-10 minutes. Some builds prioritize armor first in defensive situations, but weapons generally come first for their offensive impact. In long macro games, maintaining balanced weapon and armor upgrades is standard practice for competitive Protoss players.
