Siege Tech enables Siege Tanks to transform into a stationary artillery platform dealing 70 explosive splash damage per shot with a range of 12. In siege mode, the Tank cannot move and has a minimum firing range, but its devastating area damage defines Terran positional warfare. The transformation takes a few seconds, during which the Tank is vulnerable.

Siege Tech is foundational to Terran strategy across all matchups. In TvZ, sieged Tanks form defensive lines that Zerg must invest heavily to break. In TvP, Tank pushes supported by infantry create slow but crushing advances. In TvT, Tank positioning and siege lines are the central strategic element — games are often decided by who secures better Tank placements. The splash damage punishes clumped units severely, making it one of the most impactful abilities in the game.

Siege Tech is researched at the Machine Shop as soon as it completes in mech openings, typically around the 6-7 minute mark. In bio-mech hybrid builds, it may come slightly later. There is no competitive mech build that skips this upgrade — it is always researched immediately.
