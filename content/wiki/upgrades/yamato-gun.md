Yamato Gun is a Battlecruiser ability that fires a concentrated energy blast dealing 260 damage to a single target. It costs 150 energy per use and has a noticeable casting animation. The massive single-target damage can one-shot most units in the game and deal significant damage to structures, making it effective for sniping high-value targets like Siege Tanks, Carriers, or defensive buildings.

In competitive play, Yamato Gun is rarely seen due to the Battlecruiser's extremely high cost, slow production, and vulnerability to faster units. However, in games that reach the extreme late game, Yamato-equipped Battlecruisers can be devastating — one-shotting Defilers, Templars, or key Protoss units from long range. The ability is most relevant in TvP late-game scenarios where Battlecruisers can snipe Arbiters or in TvT where they can eliminate enemy Vessels and Tanks.

Yamato Gun is researched at the Physics Lab, which itself requires a Starport with attached Control Tower and a Science Facility. This puts it firmly in late-game territory, typically not available before the 15-20 minute mark at the earliest, and often much later if researched at all.
