Maelstrom is a Dark Archon ability that freezes all biological units in an area of effect for approximately 5 seconds. Affected units cannot move, attack, or use abilities for the duration. The stun affects both ground and air biological units, making it powerful against Zerglings, Hydralisks, Mutalisks, Marines, and Medics. Mechanical and Protoss units (which are not classified as biological) are immune.

In competitive play, Maelstrom is a niche ability due to the high cost of producing Dark Archons (requiring two Dark Templar to merge). However, it can be devastating against bio-heavy armies — stunning an entire Marine ball allows Zealots and Dragoons to close distance and engage without taking fire. Against Zerg, Maelstrom freezes Zergling swarms or Mutalisk flocks, creating a window for focused fire. It pairs well with Psionic Storm, as stunned units cannot spread to avoid the damage.

Maelstrom is researched at the Templar Archives, typically only in games where the Protoss player has committed to Dark Archon production. This is a late-game scenario, usually appearing after the 15-minute mark and most commonly seen in PvZ or PvT when facing heavy biological compositions.
