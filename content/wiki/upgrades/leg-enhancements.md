Leg Enhancements dramatically increases Zealot movement speed, transforming them from slow, easily kitable melee units into fast, threatening fighters that can chase down most ground units. The speed boost is substantial enough to change how Zealots interact with every enemy composition — they can now close gaps against retreating ranged units and surround enemies effectively.

Leg Enhancements is one of the most impactful single upgrades in the entire game. Speed Zealots are a cornerstone of Protoss strategy in every matchup. In PvT, fast Zealots can catch Marines and force engagements that Terran cannot easily kite away from. In PvZ, speed Zealots become deadly against Hydralisks and can chase down fleeing Drones. In PvP, speed Zealots dominate the ground game. The upgrade is researched in virtually every Protoss game — the strategic question is never whether to get it, but when.

Leg Enhancements is researched at the Citadel of Adun. The timing varies by matchup and build, but it is commonly started around the 8-10 minute mark. Some aggressive builds rush it earlier, while tech-heavy builds may delay it slightly. It is almost never skipped entirely.
