Each level of Ground Weapons adds +1 base attack damage to Zealots, Dragoons, Dark Templar, and Reavers. Since Zealots strike twice per attack, they effectively gain +2 damage per level. Researched at the Forge, with levels 2 and 3 requiring a Templar Archives. The upgrade also increases Reaver Scarab damage, amplifying an already devastating attack.

Ground Weapons is a high priority upgrade in gateway-heavy Protoss play across all matchups. +1 Zealots hit critical damage breakpoints against Marines and Zerglings, and +1 Dragoons trade significantly better against enemy ranged units. In PvZ, upgraded Zealots become far more cost-efficient against Zerglings. In PvP, the weapons advantage often decides Dragoon vs Dragoon battles. Pros keep the Forge continuously researching throughout mid-game.

The first level of Ground Weapons typically begins as soon as the Forge completes, which varies by build — as early as the 5-minute mark in Forge-first builds or around 7-8 minutes in gateway-first builds. Continuous upgrading through all three levels is standard in any game that reaches mid-game.
