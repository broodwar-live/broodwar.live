Each level of Ship Weapons adds +1 base attack damage to Wraiths, Valkyries, and Battlecruisers. Researched at the Armory, with higher levels requiring a Science Facility. The damage bonus applies per shot, benefiting multi-hit units like Valkyries whose rockets strike multiple times per attack cycle.

Ship Weapons is a niche upgrade in competitive play, only relevant when Terran commits to air-heavy compositions. In rare Wraith-based strategies in TvT or TvZ, upgrading ship weapons increases harassment potential against workers and key structures. Valkyrie-focused anti-air in TvZ can benefit from ship weapon upgrades when dealing with massed Mutalisks. Battlecruiser play is uncommon in high-level games, but when it appears, ship weapons significantly boost their already high damage output.

This upgrade is rarely researched in standard competitive builds. When it is, it typically comes in the late-game after ground mech upgrades are well underway, or in specialized air-transition builds. Most games end before ship weapons become a factor.
