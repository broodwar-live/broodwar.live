A 4-player space platform map created by the renowned mapmaker Earthattack (김응서) that stands as one of the most balanced and enduring maps in Brood War history. Spawns are positioned at 1, 5, 7, and 11 o'clock with rotational symmetry. Each main base leads to a cliffed natural expansion — the mineral line is exposed to high-ground harassment from above, creating a signature tactical element. Multiple pathways connect all four quadrants, including a central open area and side routes that enable flanking maneuvers.

Circuit Breaker was introduced during the late OSL/MSL era and has remained in competitive rotation ever since, appearing in nearly every ASL season. Its longevity rivals that of Fighting Spirit, and the map has hosted some of the most important matches in modern Korean Brood War. The space platform aesthetic makes it instantly recognizable, with its dark metallic bridges and platforms.

The map is widely regarded as one of the most balanced designs ever created for Brood War. All six matchups play out fairly, with no single race holding a significant statistical advantage. The cliffable naturals add tension to early-game defense — particularly for Zerg, who must watch for vulture or zealot drops onto the high ground. Terran siege pushes are effective along the main corridors, while Protoss can use the multiple paths for shuttle-based harassment. The rich expansion options ensure that games frequently reach the late stages.
