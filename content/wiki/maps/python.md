A legendary 4-player jungle map with rotational symmetry and a central plateau. Python was a cornerstone of competitive BW during the golden age of Korean pro leagues. Its large main bases, ramp-protected naturals, and open center allow for diverse strategies. The 1.3 revision is the most commonly played version.
