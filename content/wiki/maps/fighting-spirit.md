One of the most played maps in BW history. Fighting Spirit is a 4-player jungle map with a relatively standard layout — natural expansions with narrow ramps, a central open area, and multiple attack paths. Its balanced design has made it a tournament staple for over a decade. The map rewards both aggressive and defensive play styles equally.
