A 4-player twilight map that served in the ASL competitive pool from 2020 through 2023, combining modern map design principles with the atmospheric twilight tileset. The map features rotational symmetry with four spawn positions, each offering a main base connected to a natural expansion through a ramp with solid chokepoint defense. The twilight terrain provides a moody purple-blue backdrop that gives the map its distinctive visual identity.

Nostalgia's layout emphasizes balanced pathways — multiple routes connect the four quadrants, but each is clearly defined with terrain features that create natural engagement points. The center of the map serves as a contested area where armies converge, while the outer edges provide safer expansion opportunities. The standard expansion layout makes the map accessible for players of all styles, from aggressive early-game builds to patient macro strategies.

The map earned its name by evoking the spirit of classic BW map design while incorporating modern balance improvements. During its time in the ASL rotation, it produced competitive games across all matchups with no dominant race. The twilight terrain features create interesting high-ground dynamics that reward careful army positioning, and the multiple pathways prevent any single defensive setup from being unbreakable.
