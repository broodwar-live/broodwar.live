A modern 4-player badlands map introduced to the ASL competitive pool in 2024. Radeon features rotational symmetry with four spawn positions spread across the map's corners. Each main base connects to a natural expansion through a defined choke point, while the badlands terrain provides a mix of open plains and elevated ridges that create natural defensive positions.

The map's layout emphasizes positional play, with clear attack corridors leading from each base toward the center. The badlands tileset offers rugged terrain with ridgelines that siege tanks and reavers can exploit for high-ground advantage. Third base locations are accessible along the outer rim of the map, requiring players to commit army presence to defend them.

In terms of matchup balance, Radeon has shown relatively even statistics in its initial competitive seasons. The defined choke points near naturals allow Protoss to wall off effectively, while the open center terrain gives Zerg room to maneuver with mobile compositions. Terran players appreciate the positional options offered by the ridgelines. As a newer addition to the map pool, the meta is still evolving, with players continuing to discover optimal strategies for each matchup.
