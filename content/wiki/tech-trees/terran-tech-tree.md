The Terran tech tree is built around addons — specialized attachments that extend a building's capabilities. Terran is the only race whose production buildings can lift off and relocate, and the only race that requires specific addon buildings (rather than just prerequisite structures) to unlock advanced units and research.

## Base Buildings

- **Command Center** (starting building)
  - Trains: SCV (worker unit)
  - Can lift off and relocate
  - Addons: **Comsat Station**, **Nuclear Silo**
    - Comsat Station: Scanner Sweep (reveals cloaked/burrowed units)
    - Nuclear Silo: Produces nuclear missiles (requires Ghost to launch)

## Tier 1

- **Supply Depot** — provides 8 supply; required to build Barracks
- **Barracks** (requires Supply Depot)
  - Marine
  - Firebat (requires Academy)
  - Medic (requires Academy)
  - Ghost (requires Academy + Covert Ops)

## Tier 1.5

- **Refinery** — built on a Vespene Geyser; required for gas collection
- **Engineering Bay** — Infantry upgrades
  - Infantry Weapons (3 levels)
  - Infantry Armor (3 levels)
  - Also required for Missile Turret construction
- **Academy** (requires Barracks)
  - Unlocks: Firebat, Medic
  - Researches: Stim Pack, U-238 Shells (Marine range +1), Restoration (Medic), Optical Flare (Medic)

## Tier 2

- **Factory** (requires Barracks)
  - Vulture
  - Siege Tank
  - Goliath (requires Armory)
  - Addon: **Machine Shop**
    - Researches: Siege Tech (Siege Mode for Tanks), Spider Mines (Vulture), Ion Thrusters (Vulture speed)

## Tier 2.5

- **Armory** — Vehicle and Ship upgrades
  - Vehicle Weapons (3 levels)
  - Vehicle Plating (3 levels)
  - Ship Weapons (3 levels)
  - Ship Plating (3 levels)
  - Required for: Goliath, Valkyrie
- **Starport** (requires Factory)
  - Wraith
  - Dropship
  - Valkyrie (requires Armory)
  - Science Vessel (requires Science Facility)
  - Battlecruiser (requires Science Facility + Physics Lab)
  - Addon: **Control Tower**
    - Researches: Cloaking Field (Wraith cloak)

## Tier 3

- **Science Facility** (requires Starport)
  - Unlocks: Science Vessel
  - Researches: Irradiate (Science Vessel), EMP Shockwave (Science Vessel)
  - Addon: **Physics Lab**
    - Unlocks: Battlecruiser
    - Researches: Yamato Gun (Battlecruiser)
  - Addon: **Covert Ops**
    - Unlocks: Ghost
    - Researches: Lockdown (Ghost), Personnel Cloaking (Ghost cloak)

## Defensive Structures

- **Bunker** (requires Barracks) — garrisons up to 4 infantry units
- **Missile Turret** (requires Engineering Bay) — anti-air and detection

## Key Tech Paths

### Bio (Infantry)

The standard TvZ and common TvP opener. Fast, mobile, and APM-intensive.

Command Center → Supply Depot → Barracks → Academy → Stim Pack + U-238 Shells → Engineering Bay → Infantry upgrades (+1/+1, +2/+2, +3/+3)

Key units: Marine, Medic, Firebat (situational)

### Mech (Vehicle)

The dominant TvT style and a powerful TvP approach. Slower but devastating in siege positions.

Command Center → Supply Depot → Barracks → Factory → Machine Shop → Siege Tech → Armory → Vehicle upgrades

Key units: Vulture (map control, Spider Mines), Siege Tank (damage core), Goliath (anti-air support)

### Air (Starport)

Niche but powerful for specific strategies. Wraith harass, Science Vessel support, or the rare Battlecruiser play.

Command Center → Supply Depot → Barracks → Factory → Starport → Control Tower / Science Facility

Key units: Wraith (harass), Dropship (mobility), Science Vessel (Irradiate, EMP, detection), Battlecruiser (late-game)

### SK Terran (Bio + Mech hybrid)

Named after the famous Korean style. Combines bio mobility with mech firepower.

Bio core (Barracks + Academy) → Factory → Machine Shop → Siege Tank support → Starport → Science Vessel for detection and Irradiate

Key units: Marine, Medic, Siege Tank, Science Vessel
