## Overview
Patch 1.16 made StarCraft and Brood War completely free to play and made the game available for digital download. This patch is considered the definitive version of competitive Brood War — the version that all modern competitive play is based on.

## General
- StarCraft and Brood War made free to download and play
- Updated Battle.net integration
- Improved compatibility with modern operating systems
- No gameplay or balance changes

## Competitive Impact
By making the game free, Blizzard ensured BW's longevity. The Korean professional scene continued on this patch for years, and when the scene revived in 2016 with ASL, it was still Patch 1.16. All replay files from the modern era are 1.16-compatible. This patch is often called the "final" version of classic BW.
