## General

- Allied chat added
- Map download improvements
- Observer improvements for tournament play

## Terran

- Wraith air-to-air damage increased
- Goliath Charon Boosters upgrade added (+3 air range)
- Valkyrie movement speed increased

## Protoss

- Dark Templar damage increased to 40 (from 30)
- Corsair attack speed improved
- High Templar Psionic Storm no longer stacks damage

## Zerg

- Zergling Adrenal Glands upgrade moved from Lair to Hive
- Hydralisk Lurker Aspect now requires Lair (was Hive)
- Overlord base speed slightly increased

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed Dark Swarm + Lurker interaction
- Fixed various pathfinding issues
