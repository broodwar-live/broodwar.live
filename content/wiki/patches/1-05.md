## Overview

Released alongside the Brood War expansion. Added new units: Medic, Valkyrie (Terran), Dark Templar, Dark Archon, Corsair (Protoss), Lurker, Devourer (Zerg). Added new campaigns, maps, and game modes.

## New Units

- **Terran**: Medic (heals biological), Valkyrie (anti-air frigate)
- **Protoss**: Dark Templar (permanently cloaked melee), Dark Archon (spellcaster), Corsair (anti-air)
- **Zerg**: Lurker (burrowed splash), Devourer (anti-air with acid spores)

## Balance Changes

- Various unit stats adjusted for expansion balance
