## General

- Improved pathfinding for units
- Fixed various crash bugs

## Terran

- Siege Tank splash damage reduced in siege mode
- Battlecruiser Yamato Gun now costs 150 energy (was 200)

## Protoss

- Dragoon attack speed improved
- Carriers can now be upgraded to hold 8 Interceptors (from 4)

## Zerg

- Hydralisk speed upgrade cost reduced
- Lurker splash damage area slightly reduced
