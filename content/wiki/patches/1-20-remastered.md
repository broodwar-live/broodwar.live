## Overview
StarCraft: Remastered launched as a graphical and audio overhaul of the original game. It preserved the original gameplay engine exactly — the same pathfinding, unit behavior, and game mechanics — while providing HD graphics, remastered audio, and modern features.

## Graphics
- Full 4K resolution support
- Redrawn unit sprites, buildings, and terrain
- New portrait animations
- Toggle between classic and remastered graphics (F5 key)

## Audio
- Remastered music and sound effects
- Original soundtrack re-recorded
- Toggle between classic and remastered audio

## Features
- Built-in observer mode improvements
- Cloud saves for profiles, hotkeys, and replays
- Leaderboards and matchmaking rankings
- Season-based competitive ladder
- Console support (Remastered-specific skins)

## Gameplay
- **Zero gameplay changes** — the engine is identical to 1.16.1
- Same unit stats, pathfinding, frame timing
- Same 12-unit control group limit
- Same 200 supply cap
- Replays from 1.16+ remain compatible

## Competitive Impact
Remastered brought thousands of new and returning players to BW. The Korean professional scene, already revived with ASL in 2016, gained increased viewership and production value. The game's competitive integrity was preserved by keeping gameplay untouched — Remastered is a visual wrapper, not a rebalance.
