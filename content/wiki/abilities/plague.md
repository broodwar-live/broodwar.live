Plague infects all units and buildings in the target area with a deadly disease that deals 295 damage over approximately 25 seconds. Plague cannot kill — it reduces HP to a minimum of 1, leaving the unit alive but critically weakened. The damage bypasses armor and affects both shields and HP. Costs 150 energy from a Defiler and requires research at the Defiler Mound.

Plague is a devastating softening tool that transforms subsequent engagements dramatically. In ZvT, Plaguing a group of Siege Tanks leaves them at 1 HP, meaning a single Zergling attack or any splash damage kills them outright. Plagued Marines die to virtually anything, and SCVs cannot repair fast enough to offset the damage on multiple units. In ZvP, Plague strips Protoss shields first and then eats into HP, leaving expensive units like Archons and Dragoons on death's door. Combined with Dark Swarm, Plague and Consume form the trio of abilities that define Zerg's late-game dominance — Plague the army, cast Dark Swarm, then send Zerglings in to finish everything off.

To use Plague effectively, target dense clusters of high-HP units for maximum value — Siege Tanks, Carriers, and Archons are ideal targets. Remember that Plague hits buildings too, so casting it on a production cluster can deny repairs and force building losses. Avoid Plaguing units you plan to Mind Control or steal. The main counterplay is Restoration (removes Plague from one unit) and spreading units to minimize the area covered by a single cast.
