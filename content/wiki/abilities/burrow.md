Burrow allows most Zerg ground units to hide underground, becoming completely invisible to enemy units that lack detection. Burrowed units cannot move or attack, with the notable exception of Lurkers, which can only attack while burrowed. The ability is instant and can be toggled freely. Costs no energy and is researched at the Hatchery, Lair, or Hive. Units that can burrow include Zerglings, Hydralisks, Drones, Defilers, Lurkers, and Infested Terrans.

In competitive play, Burrow serves multiple strategic purposes. Burrowed Zerglings at key map positions provide free scouting and early warning. Burrowing injured units can save them from death when retreating is not possible. In ZvT, burrowed Lurkers at choke points are a fundamental defensive tool, requiring the Terran to bring detection before advancing. Burrowed Zerglings near expansions can delay enemy base construction by unburrowing to attack workers.

To use Burrow effectively, always keep a few Zerglings burrowed at watch towers and expansion locations for map awareness. Be careful not to forget about burrowed units — a common mistake is leaving Drones burrowed after dodging harassment and forgetting to unburrow them. Against opponents without detection, burrowed units at chokes can stall pushes indefinitely. Remember that Comsat scans, Observers, Overlords with detection upgrade, and Science Vessels can all reveal burrowed units.
