Yamato Cannon fires a concentrated plasma blast at a single target, dealing 260 damage. The ability has a range of 10 and a brief charging animation before firing. It deals normal damage type, meaning full damage against all armor sizes. Costs 150 energy from a Battlecruiser and requires research at the Physics Lab.

Yamato Cannon gives Battlecruisers the ability to instantly eliminate key targets before a fight begins. At 260 damage, it one-shots Defilers (80 HP), Science Vessels (150 HP), High Templar (40 HP + 40 shields), and most other spellcasters. It also deals massive damage to structures, making it effective for sniping key buildings like Spawn Pools or Gateways in a base raid. In TvP, multiple Battlecruisers with Yamato can systematically destroy Carriers by focusing fire — the Yamato deals half a Carrier's HP in a single shot.

In competitive play, Battlecruiser strategies are uncommon due to their high cost and slow build time, which limits how often Yamato Cannon appears. When Battlecruisers are on the field, prioritize Yamato shots on spellcasters and detectors first — a Yamato on a Science Vessel or Defiler removes a critical support unit. Do not waste Yamato on units that can be killed efficiently by normal attacks. Keep Battlecruisers together so their combined Yamato volleys can destroy high-HP targets. Energy management matters — let Battlecruisers build up to at least 150 energy before engaging so each one has a Yamato ready.
