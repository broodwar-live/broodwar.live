Maelstrom freezes all biological units in the target area for approximately 5 seconds, completely preventing them from moving, attacking, or using abilities. It does not affect mechanical or robotic units, and buildings are immune. Costs 100 energy from a Dark Archon and requires research at the Templar Archives.

Maelstrom is devastating against massed biological armies, particularly Zerg. A well-placed Maelstrom on a Zergling swarm or Hydralisk army leaves them completely vulnerable to Zealot charges, Psionic Storm, or Reaver Scarabs. In PvZ, the combination of Maelstrom followed by Psionic Storm is one of the most destructive spell combos in the game, as frozen units cannot flee the Storm's area of effect. Against Terran bio (Marine/Medic), Maelstrom freezes the entire infantry ball, allowing Zealots and Archons to close distance and engage on favorable terms.

The main limitation of Maelstrom is that it requires a Dark Archon, which demands heavy investment in Dark Templar tech. Since Dark Archons are created by merging two Dark Templar (each costing 125/100), the cost is steep. In practice, Maelstrom sees the most play in long PvZ games where the Protoss transitions into Dark Archon tech. Target the densest cluster of biological units, and immediately follow up with Storm or a ground assault to capitalize on the freeze window before it expires.
