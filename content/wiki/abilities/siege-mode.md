Transforms the Siege Tank into a stationary artillery platform. In Siege Mode, the tank's damage increases from 30 to 70 explosive splash damage with 12 range, but it cannot move or attack units adjacent to it. The cornerstone of Terran positional play. Researched at the Machine Shop.
