Stasis Field freezes all units — both friendly and enemy — in the target area for approximately 40 seconds. Frozen units become completely invulnerable and cannot be attacked, targeted, moved, or use abilities. The effect cannot be dispelled and lasts the full duration. Costs 100 energy from an Arbiter and requires research at the Arbiter Tribunal.

In competitive play, Stasis Field is a powerful tool for splitting enemy armies. The ideal use is to freeze part of the opponent's force and then engage the remaining unfrozen units with your full army, creating a temporary numbers advantage. In PvT, Stasis on half a Siege Tank line lets the Protoss army engage the other half without taking fire from the frozen tanks. In PvZ, it can freeze a group of Ultralisks or Defilers, removing them from the fight for 40 seconds. Stasis is also used defensively — freezing your own units that are about to die can buy time for reinforcements to arrive.

Be careful not to freeze your own units unintentionally, as the effect hits everything in the area. Avoid overlapping Stasis Field with Psionic Storm, since frozen units are invulnerable and will take no Storm damage. The best Stasis casts isolate a specific portion of the enemy army — cast on their reinforcements as they arrive, or on their back line while your army engages their front. Practice the timing to avoid wasting Arbiter energy on suboptimal casts.
