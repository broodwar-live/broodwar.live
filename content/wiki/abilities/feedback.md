Feedback drains all remaining energy from a target unit and deals damage equal to the amount of energy drained. A full-energy Science Vessel (200 energy) takes 200 damage, which can destroy it outright. Costs 50 energy from a Dark Archon and requires no research — it is available immediately upon merging two Dark Templar into a Dark Archon.

Feedback is a powerful anti-spellcaster tool that can instantly eliminate or severely damage key support units. Its best targets are units with high maximum energy: Science Vessels (200 energy, 150 HP — a full-energy Vessel dies instantly), Defilers (200 energy), Queens, High Templar, and Medics. In PvT, Feedback on a Science Vessel prevents EMP and Irradiate while dealing lethal damage. In PvZ, it can snipe full-energy Defilers before they cast Dark Swarm or Plague.

Despite its effectiveness, Feedback is rarely seen in competitive play because Dark Archons themselves are expensive and difficult to produce — requiring two Dark Templar to merge. The opportunity cost of investing in Dark Templar tech plus the merge time makes them impractical in most builds. When you do have Dark Archons, prioritize Feedback on the highest-energy enemy spellcaster before using Mind Control or Maelstrom, as it both removes a threat and deals significant damage simultaneously.
