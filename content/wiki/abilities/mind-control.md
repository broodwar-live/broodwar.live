Permanently takes control of a target enemy unit. The Dark Archon's shields are reduced to 0 upon casting. Mind Control can steal workers (enabling multi-race production), detectors, or expensive units. One of the rarest but most dramatic abilities in competitive play. Researched at the Templar Archives.
