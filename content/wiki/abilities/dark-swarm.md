Creates a dark cloud that blocks all ranged attacks against ground units underneath it. Units inside Dark Swarm can still attack normally, and melee units are unaffected. This is arguably the most powerful Zerg ability — it completely negates Marine, Dragoon, and Hydralisk fire. Researched at the Defiler Mound.
