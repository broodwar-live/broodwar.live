Consume instantly kills a single friendly Zerg unit to restore 50 energy to the casting Defiler. The targeted unit is destroyed immediately regardless of its HP or type — any Zerg unit can be consumed, from a Zergling to an Ultralisk. Costs no energy to cast and requires research at the Defiler Mound.

Consume is the engine that powers the Defiler's entire spell arsenal. Without Consume, a Defiler can cast Dark Swarm once (100 energy) or cannot even afford Plague (150 energy) without building up energy over time. With Consume, a Defiler can consume two Zerglings (worth 50 minerals total) and immediately have enough energy for Dark Swarm, then consume two more for Plague. This makes Defiler spellcasting effectively unlimited as long as sacrificial units are available. In competitive ZvT, Zerg players keep a steady supply of Zerglings near their Defilers specifically as Consume fodder.

The key to efficient Consume usage is always having cheap units nearby for the Defiler to eat. Rally Zerglings to your Defiler's position or use a control group with both Defilers and Zerglings together. Never consume expensive units unless absolutely necessary — Zerglings at 25 minerals per pair are the standard sacrifice. A critical mistake is allowing your Defiler to run out of Consume targets mid-engagement, leaving it unable to cast additional Dark Swarms when the first expires. Always bring more Zerglings than you think you need.
