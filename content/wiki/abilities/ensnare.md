Ensnare slows all units in the target area, reducing both their movement speed and attack speed significantly for approximately 55 seconds. It also reveals cloaked and burrowed units in the area, removing their invisibility for the duration. Costs 75 energy from a Queen and requires research at the Queen's Nest.

Ensnare is a versatile debuff that can swing engagements by crippling enemy mobility and damage output. Against Terran bio pushes, an Ensnared Marine ball moves and attacks significantly slower, making them far less threatening and easier to surround with Zerglings. The ability also counters cloaked Wraiths, removing their stealth without requiring an Overlord or Spore Colony in position. In ZvP, Ensnare on a Zealot/Dragoon army reduces their ability to close distance or retreat, making them vulnerable to Lurker spines and Zergling surround.

Despite its strong effect, Ensnare is rarely seen in competitive play because Queens themselves are uncommon — they are expensive, fragile, and the production slot could be used for more immediately impactful units. When you do invest in a Queen, Ensnare pairs well with ambush tactics. Cast it on an incoming push before engaging to maximize its value. The reveal effect makes it a useful counter to Dark Templar harass if Observer or scan detection is not available. Stack Ensnare with other slowing effects or area control spells for devastating combo potential.
