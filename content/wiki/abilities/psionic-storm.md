Psionic Storm unleashes a devastating energy storm in the target area, dealing 112 total damage over approximately 3 seconds to all ground and air units caught within it. The damage ignores armor upgrades entirely. Multiple storms cast on the same area do not stack — units can only take damage from one storm at a time (since Patch 1.08). Costs 75 energy from a High Templar and requires research at the Templar Archives.

Psionic Storm is widely considered the most powerful area damage spell in Brood War. It is the backbone of Protoss mid-to-late game in every matchup. In PvT, Storm drops via Shuttle are a core strategy — loading High Templar into a Shuttle, flying over a Marine/Medic army, and dropping storms can annihilate an entire bio force in seconds. In PvZ, storms zone out Hydralisk armies and punish Mutalisk stacking. Even in PvP, well-placed storms on Dragoon clusters can swing fights decisively.

The key to effective Storm usage is casting on units that are clumped together and cannot easily retreat. Since storms do not stack, spread multiple storms across the enemy army rather than overlapping them on a single area. Storm drops require precise Shuttle micro — drop the Templar, cast immediately, and pick them up before they die. Counter Storm by spreading your units to minimize splash overlap and by targeting the High Templar or their Shuttle before they can cast.
