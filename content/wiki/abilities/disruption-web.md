Disruption Web projects an energy field onto the ground that completely prevents any ground unit or building beneath it from attacking for approximately 20 seconds. Affected units can still move out of the area, but stationary defenses are effectively disabled for the duration. Costs 125 energy from a Corsair and requires research at the Fleet Beacon.

Disruption Web is a core ability in PvZ Corsair-based strategies. Its primary use is disabling Zerg static defenses — casting it on Spore Colonies allows Corsairs to operate freely over Zerg bases, picking off Overlords and harassing workers. Against Terran, Disruption Web on Siege Tanks or Bunkers nullifies their fire, enabling a ground assault. It can also be used on Missile Turrets to allow Corsair or Scout flybys without taking anti-air fire.

In competitive PvZ, Corsair openings often revolve around using Disruption Web aggressively. Once Spore Colonies are disabled, Corsairs can dominate the airspace and deny Overlord positioning, which limits the Zerg's supply and scouting. To use it well, target clumps of static defenses or Siege Tank formations rather than isolated units. Remember that mobile units can walk out of the web, so it is most effective against immobile buildings and sieged tanks. Chain multiple Disruption Webs to cover a wide area during a push. Avoid casting it where your own ground units need to fight, as it affects friendly units too.
