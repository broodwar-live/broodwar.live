Spawn Broodling instantly kills a single non-robotic ground unit and creates two Broodlings in its place. The Broodlings are small, temporary melee units that fight for the Zerg player and die after approximately 120 seconds. The ability bypasses all armor, shields, and HP — no unit can survive it regardless of its stats. Costs 150 energy from a Queen and requires research at the Queen's Nest. It cannot target robotic units (Reavers, Dragoons, etc.), buildings, or air units.

In competitive play, Spawn Broodling is a devastating anti-siege tool. Its most impactful use is instantly removing a sieged Siege Tank — the most dangerous Terran ground unit — without any counterplay. In ZvT, a single Queen can systematically eliminate a Tank line one at a time, and because Queens fly, they can approach from angles that ground units cannot. The ability also one-shots High Templar, Defilers, and any other high-value biological ground unit. Some Zerg players use it in ZvP to snipe Reavers before they can fire their Scarabs.

Queens are fragile and rarely produced in competitive play, which limits how often Spawn Broodling appears. To use it well, fly the Queen in from a safe angle, cast on the highest-value target, and retreat immediately. Pairing Queens with Overlord vision ensures you can target the right unit. The biggest mistake is losing the Queen before casting — always keep Queens away from anti-air and only commit them when you have a clear target.
