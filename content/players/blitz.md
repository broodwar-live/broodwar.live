Active in BSL qualifying rounds and European online tournaments, he brings solid mechanical play and a willingness to compete against the best international players. His TvZ features standard mech approaches executed with clean fundamentals, while his TvP shows improving timing attack execution. As one of Finland's most active competitive players, Blitz helps maintain the Nordic presence in the international scene and contributes to the diversity of the European BW community.
