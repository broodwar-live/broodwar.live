Known as "The Emperor," Boxer is the founding father of professional StarCraft and the single most important figure in establishing Brood War as a cultural phenomenon in South Korea. He won the first two OnGameNet Starleague championships and became the face of Korean esports during its explosive growth in the early 2000s. His playstyle was defined by innovative tactics and showmanship — he pioneered numerous Terran strategies including creative uses of dropships and unconventional army compositions that caught opponents off guard. Beyond his individual titles, Boxer built the legendary SK Telecom T1 dynasty. Though long retired from competition, his impact on the game is immeasurable. He proved that esports could produce genuine celebrities, paving the way for every pro gamer who followed.
