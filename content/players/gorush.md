GoRush is a Korean Zerg competing in the modern scene with a focus on aggressive play and early-game pressure. His ZvP features zergling-based pressure builds and quick lurker timings that aim to end games before Protoss can establish their late-game army. GoRush is active in ASL qualifiers and online tournaments, where his aggressive approach can produce quick victories. His willingness to commit to all-in strategies makes him volatile but dangerous, and his games are typically fast-paced and decisive.
