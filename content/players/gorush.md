GoRush is a Korean Zerg competing in the modern scene with a focus on aggressive play and early-game pressure that aims to end games decisively. His ZvP features zergling-based pressure builds and quick lurker timings that aim to end games before Protoss can establish their late-game army. GoRush is active in ASL qualifiers and online tournaments, where his aggressive approach can produce quick victories against opponents who fail to scout his intentions. His willingness to commit to all-in strategies makes him volatile but dangerous.
