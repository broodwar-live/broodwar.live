Widely regarded as the greatest Brood War player of all time, Flash has dominated competitive StarCraft for over fifteen years. Known as the "God of BW," he holds a record eight ASL titles along with multiple OSL championships, cementing his status as the most decorated player in history. His playstyle centers on impeccable macro, unmatched multitasking, and relentless mech play that redefined TvZ at the highest level. Flash's mechanical precision and strategic depth made him virtually unbeatable during his peak years, and his legendary rivalry with Jaedong produced some of the most iconic matches in esports history. He remains active in the modern ASL era and continues to stream, drawing large audiences who marvel at his enduring skill.
