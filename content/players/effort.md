EffOrt is an elite Zerg player who has been a fixture in the competitive scene from the golden era through the modern ASL. His ZvP is widely considered among the best in the world, featuring precise lurker positioning and smart defiler play that make his late-game armies incredibly difficult to engage. EffOrt has reached multiple ASL semifinals and has taken sets off the very best players, demonstrating that his skill remains at an elite level. His macro management is exceptionally clean, and his ability to maintain drone production while fending off aggression shows deep understanding of Zerg's economic mechanics. He continues to be a dangerous opponent for any player in the modern scene.
