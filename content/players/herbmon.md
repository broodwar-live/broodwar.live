Herbmon is a Canadian Zerg and one of the stronger North American players in the international BW scene. Active in BSL events and international online tournaments, he brings solid Zerg fundamentals and a competitive mindset to every match. His ZvT is his most developed matchup, with strong mutalisk control and good macro management that allow him to compete with the top European players. Herbmon's consistent participation helps maintain North America's presence in the international scene.
