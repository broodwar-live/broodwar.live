XellOs is a legendary Protoss from the golden era who achieved significant tournament success with a flashy, aggressive playstyle. His approach to the game was defined by bold attacks and decisive aggression that could overwhelm opponents before they had time to react. XellOs's PvZ was particularly celebrated, featuring sharp timing attacks and creative uses of dark templar and corsairs that could swing games in an instant. He won multiple major tournament titles and was considered one of the most dangerous players to face in a best-of-one format, where his aggression was hardest to prepare for. His exciting style made him a fan favorite, and his impact on aggressive Protoss play continues to be felt in how modern players approach the race.
