Rain is the most accomplished Protoss player of the modern era, holding six ASL titles and a KSL championship that place him among the greatest players in Brood War history. His rivalry with Flash has defined competitive BW for the past decade, producing countless iconic finals that showcase the highest level of PvT play. Rain's signature style revolves around precise storm drops, aggressive gateway pressure, and clutch performances in elimination matches that have earned him a reputation as a big-game player. His ability to read opponent strategies and adjust mid-series is unmatched among active Protoss players. Beyond his individual brilliance, Rain's sustained excellence across dozens of ASL seasons demonstrates a level of consistency that few players in any era have achieved. He remains the gold standard for modern Protoss play.
