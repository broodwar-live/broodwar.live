Motive is a Korean Zerg active in the modern scene with solid fundamentals and a preference for macro-oriented play that prioritizes economic efficiency. His ZvP is his most reliable matchup, where patient economic development and effective lurker usage create a strong foundation for late-game army compositions. Motive competes in ASL qualifiers and Korean online tournaments, bringing consistency to every series. His macro management is clean and his defensive play is solid, making him a difficult opponent for players who rely on early aggression to win.
