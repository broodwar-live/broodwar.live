Known as "The Tyrant," Jaedong is one of the greatest Zerg players in Brood War history and Flash's most famous rival. An OSL and MSL champion, his peak years in the late 2000s saw him dominate with a combination of ferocious mutalisk micro, aggressive zergling control, and strategic brilliance that overwhelmed opponents across all matchups. Jaedong's mutalisk micro was legendary — his ability to dance mutalisks around enemy defenses while simultaneously managing his macro set a standard that few have matched. The Flash-Jaedong rivalry transcended the game itself, becoming a cultural phenomenon in Korean esports. Though less active in the modern era, his legacy as one of the two greatest players ever — and the greatest Zerg — endures as a cornerstone of competitive BW history.
