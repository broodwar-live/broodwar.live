His playstyle is built on solid fundamentals and reliable mechanics, with a particular emphasis on safe macro openings that allow him to reach the mid-game in strong positions. While he has yet to break through to the main ASL bracket consistently, his persistence and steady improvement reflect the depth of talent in the Korean Terran scene. Action's TvZ features disciplined siege tank play and careful expansion timing, making him a difficult opponent for Zerg players who rely on early aggression.
