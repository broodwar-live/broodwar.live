Bonyth is a Polish Protoss and one of the strongest international players in Brood War history. A multiple-time BSL champion and perennial finalist, he has dominated the European scene with his precise storm play and excellent PvT that can challenge even Korean-level Terrans. Bonyth's mechanical skill is exceptional for a non-Korean player, and his consistency across BSL seasons is unmatched in the international community. His PvZ is also strong, featuring smart army compositions and efficient macro that keep Zerg opponents under constant pressure. As one of Poland's BW legends alongside Scan and Trutacz, Bonyth has been instrumental in establishing Poland as the premier non-Korean BW nation.
