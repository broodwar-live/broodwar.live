Light is a mechanically gifted Terran who has been a consistent performer throughout the ASL era, known for his strong bio play and excellent marine-medic control. His TvZ is his signature matchup, where his crisp micro and aggressive timing attacks have taken down some of the best Zerg players in the scene. Light's APM is among the highest of active Terran players, and his speed allows him to execute multi-pronged attacks that overwhelm opponents. He has reached the ASL Ro8 on multiple occasions and is always considered a dangerous opponent in any bracket. His commitment to bio-heavy strategies gives his games a fast-paced, action-packed quality that makes him a fan favorite among viewers.
