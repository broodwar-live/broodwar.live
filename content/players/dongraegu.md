DongRaeGu is a legendary Zerg who made his name in both StarCraft II and Brood War, demonstrating exceptional versatility across two different games. In BW, his Zerg play features intelligent macro management and a deep understanding of the race's complex mid-game transitions. DongRaeGu's SC2 achievements include major tournament victories, and he brought that championship-level mental fortitude to his BW competition. His ZvT is his strongest BW matchup, where his experience with long macro games gives him an edge in late-game decision-making. He has participated in ASL events and remains a respected figure whose dual-game career is nearly unique in the competitive scene.
