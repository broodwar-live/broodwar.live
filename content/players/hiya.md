HiyA is a Korean Terran active in the modern competitive scene, recognized for his strong TvT play and aggressive drop-based strategies that keep opponents reacting. His command of the mirror matchup is his greatest asset, featuring patient siege lines and well-timed dropship harass that create constant pressure across the map. HiyA has appeared in multiple ASL qualifying rounds and has taken sets off higher-ranked players with his opportunistic playstyle. His willingness to commit to aggressive openings in TvP also gives him upset potential against Protoss opponents who expect standard play. He is a solid middle-tier competitor who adds depth to the Korean Terran scene.
