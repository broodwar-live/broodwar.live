Spark is a Japanese Zerg representing one of the smaller but dedicated Asian BW communities outside Korea. Active in international online tournaments, he brings solid fundamentals and enthusiasm for the game from Japan's competitive scene. His ZvT is his most practiced matchup, with developing mutalisk control and improving macro management. Spark's regular participation helps maintain Japan's presence in the global BW community.
