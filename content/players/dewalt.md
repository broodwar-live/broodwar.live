Dewalt's mechanical skill rivals that of top Korean professionals, and his consistency in BSL events is unmatched. Beyond his individual dominance, he has elevated the standard of international BW competition, forcing other top players to raise their level to compete. His legacy as the greatest non-Korean player of all time appears secure, and he remains the measuring stick against which all international players are judged.
