Cipher is a Portuguese Terran active in the European Brood War scene, competing in BSL qualifying rounds and regional online tournaments. He represents the growing Southern European BW community with a playstyle that features disciplined mechanics and a focus on standard Terran strategies. His TvZ is his most developed matchup, with patient siege tank play that reflects good understanding of the positional aspects of the matchup. Cipher's regular participation in international events helps grow the game's reach in Portugal and the Iberian Peninsula.
