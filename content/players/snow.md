Snow is one of the premier Protoss players of the modern era, with multiple ASL titles that cement his status as an elite competitor. His defensive playstyle is his hallmark — Snow excels at weathering early aggression, stabilizing his economy, and then overwhelming opponents with superior late-game army compositions. His PvT is particularly feared, where his ability to survive Terran timing attacks and transition into powerful carrier or arbiter-based armies has produced numerous comeback victories. Snow's game sense and composure under pressure are exceptional, rarely making the panicked decisions that cost other players games in high-stakes situations. He is a consistent ASL finalist and one of the most reliable performers in the modern scene, representing the patient, strategic school of Protoss play.
