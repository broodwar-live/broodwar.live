Reach is a legendary Protoss who was considered one of the most complete players during the golden era of Brood War. His playstyle combined strong macro fundamentals with excellent micro control, making him dangerous in every phase of the game. Reach's PvT was especially formidable, featuring aggressive zealot-dragoon play in the early game that transitioned smoothly into powerful late-game armies with arbiters and high templar. He reached multiple OSL and MSL finals, and his consistent presence among the top players of his era demonstrated remarkable longevity. Known for his calm demeanor and focused concentration during matches, Reach embodied the ideal of the well-rounded Protoss player. His contributions to the development of Protoss strategy continue to influence how the race is played today.
