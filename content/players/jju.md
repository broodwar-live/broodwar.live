JJu is a Korean Protoss competing in the modern scene with an emphasis on macro play and late-game army compositions. His patient approach allows him to reach the powerful carrier and arbiter phases of Protoss play, where his army becomes very difficult to deal with. JJu's PvT benefits most from this approach, as his ability to survive early Terran aggression and transition into late-game fleets has produced several notable victories. He is active in ASL qualifiers and online tournaments, where his patience and macro focus distinguish him from more aggressive Protoss competitors.
