A high-variance PvP build that rushes to dark templar archives, betting that the opponent has not built a robotics facility for an observer. In the mirror matchup, detection depends entirely on observers — there are no other Protoss detection options on the ground. If the opponent went double gateway or fast expand without robotics, DTs are invisible and lethal.

Rush citadel of Adun and templar archives as fast as possible. Send DTs into the opponent's base the moment they spawn. If there is no observer, target probes first to cripple the economy, then production buildings. Always have a follow-up plan — merge DTs into archons and expand if the opponent survives.

Best used when you scout no robotics facility from your opponent. It is a gamble: instant win or instant disadvantage depending entirely on what the opponent builds. Weak against any build that includes early robotics, as the observer shuts down the entire strategy. On maps with easy scouting, opponents are more likely to see the archives in time to react.
