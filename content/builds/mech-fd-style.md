Flash's signature TvP mech approach, emphasizing vulture map control combined with precise siege tank positioning. The core idea is to contain Protoss on fewer bases while you take the map, using vulture speed to deny expansions and spider mines to create safe zones for your tank lines.

Execution requires excellent vulture micro — individual vultures patrol expansion paths, plant mines at key positions, and pick off stray probes and zealots. Meanwhile, siege tanks push forward incrementally, always in siege mode with overlapping fire coverage. Add science vessels for defensive matrix on key tanks and to detect observers.

This build shines on maps with open ground where vultures thrive, and against Protoss players who struggle with multi-front pressure. Transition into late-game goliath/tank with science vessel support. Weak against mass carriers if the game extends too long without enough goliath production, and against aggressive storm drops that disrupt tank formations.
