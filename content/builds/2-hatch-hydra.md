An aggressive two-base hydralisk timing attack in ZvP. The idea is to mass hydralisks from two hatcheries and push across the map before Protoss has access to psionic storm, which devastates clumped hydra groups. This is an all-in build — if the push fails, Zerg is behind on economy with no third base.

Execute by building a hydralisk den as soon as lair tech is available, then produce hydralisks non-stop from both hatcheries. Research range and speed upgrades. Push with roughly 20 hydralisks plus zergling support. Micro is critical — spread hydras to minimize zealot splash and focus fire dragoons.

Best on maps with short rush distances where the timing attack arrives before storm. Use this when you scout a greedy Protoss opening like fast third base. Transition by adding a third hatchery and teching to mutalisks if the push does significant damage but does not kill. Weak against Protoss who rush storm tech or have early reaver defense, and on large maps where the travel time gives Protoss time to prepare.
