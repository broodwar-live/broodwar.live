A more aggressive alternative to the standard three hatch mutalisk build in ZvT. By skipping the third hatchery, you get mutalisks on the map faster — often before Terran has built enough missile turrets to protect all mineral lines. The trade-off is a weaker economy, making this a build that needs to do damage to justify itself.

The key timing: your first 5-6 mutalisks should hit Terran's base while turret coverage is incomplete. Focus on sniping SCVs and exposed siege tanks. Keep mutalisk count growing and harass multiple locations to stretch Terran's defense thin. Avoid clumping mutas into turret fire.

Best used when you scout a greedy Terran who delays engineering bay, or on maps where you can reach the opponent's mineral lines quickly. Transition by adding the third hatchery behind mutalisk pressure, then tech into lurkers for ground defense. Weak if Terran goes fast engineering bay with multiple turrets, as your smaller economy cannot sustain repeated mutalisk losses against prepared defenses.
