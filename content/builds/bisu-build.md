Named after Bisu, who perfected this strategy in his legendary 2007 OSL championship run against sAviOr, fundamentally shifting PvZ from Zerg-favored to balanced. The build opens with stargate into corsairs, which clear overlords across the map to remove Zerg's mobile detection. Dark templar then walk into unprotected bases for devastating kills.

The critical sequence: produce 3-4 corsairs first to hunt overlords, especially near Zerg's third base and natural. Time the dark templar arrival with overlord kills — if detection is gone, DTs can wipe out entire mineral lines. Keep corsairs active to deny new overlords from reaching key areas.

This build works on any map but excels where overlord positioning is predictable. Transition into corsair/reaver for continued pressure, or shift to high templar with storm for the late game. Weak against early spore colony placement at each base, or Zerg players who keep overlords safely stacked and produce extras proactively.
